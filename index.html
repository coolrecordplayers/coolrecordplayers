Are you looking to buy a turntable ? There are many different options . In this article we will look at the options and look for the benefit of each turntable . In the end, you will discover exactly what will make the best purchase and how to find them .

Readers have been around for decades . First start with gramophones , which rapidly progressed . The gramophone then gradually became types of turntable . Vinyl had a strong favorite turntable that worked with vinyl .

Today they are still the favorites , even among top DJs . The record player lets you listen to music . At this point , there are two paths you may need a turntable . If you have a stereo and want to have a record player to play music , then this is an option , if you want to mix music, then you need two turntables .

The new turntables work with CD. So there are two options for this. You could go for vinyl turntables or CD versions . This will be based on your own needs more than anything .

You will find the belt drive and direct drive versions . The belt drive versions are great if you're really on a budget or just want to play music . To mix the direct drive versions are best suited .
